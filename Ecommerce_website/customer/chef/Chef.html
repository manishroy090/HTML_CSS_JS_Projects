Paul Thompson

SECOND CHEF

Paul was born in London and has
been working as a chef since he
was 17 years old.

His loves football, is a big fan of
Tottenham Hotspur, horse
racing and has a genuine passion
for cooking.

Paul's favourite dish is a classic
Chateaubriand with chateau
potatoes seasonal vegetables
and the traditional
accompaniment - bearnaise
sauce.

He enjoys the tranquillity of
Guernsey, cliff walking with
spectacular views, the
friendliness of people and the
climate that island offers
throughout the year.
